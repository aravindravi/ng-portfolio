Chatify

CONTEXT

Chatify, a UK based startup was looking to connect consumers with local businesses through Instant Messaging.

TEAM

Theepan Vela (Founder), myself (UX Designer) and a few Android developers

MY ROLES

  • Interaction Design

  • Visual Design

PROBLEMS SOLVED

  • Application Redesign

  • Designing New Features

  • Improving Brand Credibility

  • Improving Customer Experience

USER RESEARCH

User Research and Market Research were conducted by the client prior to creating the business model so I wasn't involved in this part. However, I documented Personas to have a clear understanding of who I was designing for.

  • Personas

    Based on the Business Model the client described, I documented 2 personas

  • Storyboards

    Storyboard helped prioritize the more important features and justify decisions on interaction design

  • Mindset Maps

    Mindset maps helped document the various thoughts people had at every touchpoint with the Chatify service

STORYBOARD

Here is a storyboard depicting the main features that drive the business for Chatify.

USER STORIES

  • As a customer, I want to get in touch with local businesses in order to get quick responses to the questions I have

  • As a customer, I want in order to find local deals best suited for me to make cheap purchases

  • As a business owner, I want to respond to my customers in order to improve my customer service

  • As a business owner, I want to put up deals and discounts in order to promote my business

USABILITY TESTING

Usability testing was done inhouse with the other team members due to budget constraints. However, the plan is to use analytics to study customer touchpoints once the app is launched.

BRANDING

The previous visual design was lacking in proper branding. I redid the logos, color and typography. I did try to convince the client to change the hue from Teal to a tint of Blue but was unsuccessful. Teal is a nice color but for this application, a brighter color would suit better as it would be more recognizable and attractive, especially for the audience. It also reflects the brand better for the tenets that the client discussed.
