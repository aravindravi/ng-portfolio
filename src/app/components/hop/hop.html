CONTEXT

In Feb 2015, I was given a design challenge by Idean, a design agency in the US - make grocery shopping fun, efficient and meaningful. The solution I presented got me the internship offer from Idean.

THE DESIGN CHALLENGE

"How might we make the grocery shopping experience more ..."

Efficient

Meaningful

Fun

HOW I SAW THE PROBLEM

There were numerous mobile applications out there that attempted to solve this problem. Before diving into the solution, it was important to validate if this was a real problem that I was solving. (Spoiler alert: Turned out that it was). I had to consider different constraints to analyze the problem.

  • Is this a real problem and is it worth solving?

  • How can research inform the design?

  • What are competitor strengths and weaknesses?

  • What is the contextual relevance?

  • How to involve potential users in the research?

  • How can success be measured?
