Cost of living in USA

Summary

Information Visualization using Tableau and COLI dataset sponsored by Council for Community and Economic Research.

Team

Wenvi Hidayat, Clem Auyeung, Jana Uhrmeister and myself.

My Roles

  • Interaction Design

  • Information Visualization

  • Programming

Design Question

How might we help international students better understand the cost of living in USA?

Process

THE FINAL PRODUCT

The data was presented as a visualization enabling users to explore the cost of living across different cities, with a degree of customization and personalization. The Cost of Living enables users to filter and obtain details on-demand. The comparison screen allowed users to compare costs across different cities.
